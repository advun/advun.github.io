CRIMSON Power Management IC

A power management ASIC designed to harvest, store, and programmably output solar energy to edge devices. Built-in safety and power-saving features protect the chip and downstream loads under variable energy conditions. Won second place among WPI Major Qualifying Projects and taped out on the TSMC 180nm process in May 2026.

Tensor Coprocessor for Croc SoC

A high speed systolic array for the Croc SoC, capable of 160 million MAC operations a second. A custom software driver and output queues were designed to manage strict data tranfer throughput constraints. This project was taped out on the TSMC180nm process in April 2026.

Trace Data Compressor

Developed a bus data compressor to extend the period of time bus activity on an FPGA or ASIC could be recorded. Designed to be as simple a compression algorithm as possible, to take up as little space as possible on the chip. Achieved a 7.4x lossless compression rate on real bus data, taking up just 352 logic cells and 51 flip flops. Originally developed for GoatHacks 2026, and took home Best Rookie Hack. A later, improved version was taped out with Tiny Tapeout in April 2026.
