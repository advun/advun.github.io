## Outside the Lab
I have been fortunate to serve several times as a Teaching Assistant for many of the Digital Design courses at WPI, mentoring students through the fundamentals of digital logic.  

I also served as WPI's Spacecraft Development Club's Support Systems Subteam Lead, designing the main PCB and the systems that will control WPI's first satellite.  Through SDC and IEEE, I ran several workshops teaching basic circuit design to students from Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering backgrounds.

When not working, I am an avid long distance backpacker, having hiked the entire Appalachian Trail from Georgia to Maine
in the summer of 2024.  I also enjoy baking, rowing, and complex board games, which I occasionally try my hand at creating.
